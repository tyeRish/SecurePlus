PRIVACY POLICY

EFFECTIVE DATE: MARCH 2026  ·  SEC+ OPERATOR

Short version: SEC+ Operator collects no personal data, has no accounts, no servers, no tracking, and no ads. Everything stays on your device.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Nothing. SEC+ Operator does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

The app stores your quiz progress (score, streak, missed questions) in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

ACCOUNTS & REGISTRATION

No account is required. No email address, name, or any personal information is ever requested.

INTERNET & NETWORK ACCESS

The app requests internet access solely to load web fonts (Google Fonts) on first launch. No user data is sent during this request. The app functions fully offline after initial load — fonts are cached by the browser/webview.

The encryption visualizer uses the device's built-in Web Crypto API. All cryptographic operations run locally on your device. No keys, plaintexts, or ciphertexts are ever transmitted anywhere.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

SEC+ Operator does not use analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.

The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) for typography. Google's privacy policy governs that request: policies.google.com/privacy.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

SEC+ Operator does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no children's data to be concerned about.

DATA SECURITY

Since we collect no personal data and have no servers, there is no user data at risk. Your quiz progress lives only in your device's local storage and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Given the nature of this app (no data collection), changes are unlikely.

CONTACT

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact TundraCore IT via github.com/tyeRish/SecurePlus.
